Manatee

A large, fully aquatic, mostly herbivorous marine mammals sometimes known as sea cows.

  • Scientific Name: Trichechidae
  • Measure: up to 4.0 metres long
  • Average weigh: 590 kilograms
  • Living species: the Amazonian manatee, the West Indian manatee and the West African manatee

Manatees are occasionally called sea cows, as they are slow plant-eaters, peaceful and similar to cows on land. They have paddle-like flippers.
